# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Travellers (couples, solo, families with one child) evaluating a 2-guest short-stay flat in Nicosia (EN), Sicily. Most arrive on mobile from Booking/Airbnb listings, Google Maps or word of mouth, and are deciding whether to book direct. Languages: Italian primary; EN/DE/FR/ES secondary. Context: quick evaluation on a phone — photos first, then price, reviews, location, and a frictionless way to ask for dates.

## Product Purpose

A single-property direct-booking site for "La Mansarda" (one-bedroom flat, 4th floor no lift, max 2 guests, €49–59/night). Success = a visitor sends a date request via WhatsApp or email. The site must beat the OTA listing on trust (real reviews, CIN IT086012C2KM8BBUCU, named owner, clear policies) and on price ("book direct, no fees").

## Brand Personality

Warm, genuine, local. A family-run home in an authentic Sicilian mountain town — not a hotel, not a startup. Quietly confident: photography-led, restrained, trustworthy. The host's hospitality (welcome kit, breakfast, guitar and chess in the flat) is the brand.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI landing-page aesthetics (hero-metric templates, identical card grids, gradient text).
- Spectacle that doesn't serve a booking decision: no 3D, no video, no heavy animation libraries.
- OTA clutter (urgency banners, fake scarcity, popups).
- Corporate/luxury-hotel coldness; this is a 50 m² mansarda, the tone must stay personal.

## Design Principles

1. **Photography decides, UI assists** — the flat and Nicosia sell the stay; chrome stays quiet.
2. **"Prenota" always reachable** — on mobile the request-dates path is never more than one tap away.
3. **Trust is content** — real reviews with sources, CIN, named owner, honest constraints (4 flights of stairs, strict cancellation) stated plainly.
4. **Fast and frugal** — one static HTML file, no frameworks, no build step; keep it that way. Performance is a feature.
5. **Restraint over novelty** — micro-animations only where they aid scanning or feedback; identity (Playfair Display + Inter, navy/azure) is established and preserved.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Lighthouse accessibility ≥ 95. WCAG AA contrast everywhere, visible focus states, semantic landmarks, full keyboard operability (incl. lightbox), `prefers-reduced-motion` honoured, touch targets ≥ 44px. Multilingual content must stay in sync across IT/EN/DE/FR/ES.
